We're getting involved!

Spring Garden Festival (April 2023)

Spring Garden Festival

Bloom Valley Nursery proudly hosted the annual Spring Garden Festival, bringing together gardening enthusiasts for a weekend of workshops, plant sales, and expert-led discussions on sustainable gardening. Attendees enjoyed live demonstrations on composting, pollinator-friendly planting, and seasonal garden planning, all while exploring a diverse selection of locally grown plants.

Community Tree Planting Initiative

In collaboration with local schools and environmental organizations, Bloom Valley Nursery organized a Community Tree Planting Initiative to enhance green spaces around our town. Volunteers of all ages came together to plant native trees and shrubs, reinforcing our commitment to sustainability and urban reforestation.

Garden to Table Workshop

March 1st, 2025

Join us for an interactive workshop where local chefs and gardening experts will teach attendees how to grow and cook with fresh herbs and vegetables. Learn about container gardening, organic soil preparation, and how to create delicious meals straight from your backyard.

Family Pollinator Day

June 7th, 2025

A fun-filled day dedicated to educating families about the importance of pollinators in our ecosystem! Enjoy kid-friendly activities like building bee hotels, butterfly garden tours, and hands-on flower planting sessions. Plus, take home a free pollinator-friendly plant to start your own garden!

Holiday Wreath-Making Workshop

November 24th, 2025

Get into the holiday spirit with our annual Wreath-Making Workshop! Using fresh greenery from our nursery, participants will create beautiful, handcrafted wreaths to decorate their homes for the season. Hot cocoa and festive treats will be provided, making it a perfect event for friends and family.
